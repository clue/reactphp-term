## 1.3.0 (2020-11-06)

    Prepare PHP 8 support, update to PHPUnit 9 and simplify test matrix.
    (#29 and #30 by @SimonFrings)

    (#27 by @clue)

*   Update project homepage.
    (#26 by @clue)

*   Feature: Forward compatibility with Stream v1.0 and v0.7 (while keeping BC)
